From Humble Beginnings to Industry Leader

Founded in 2010 by automotive enthusiasts with a vision for premium car rentals, Veloce started with just three vehicles in a small garage. Today, we've grown to a fleet of over 500 meticulously maintained vehicles serving customers in 15 cities nationwide.

Our commitment to quality, transparency, and exceptional customer service has earned us numerous industry awards and the trust of over 100,000 satisfied customers.
