Complete Terms & Conditions

Review the complete terms and conditions for our airplane rental services. Please read carefully before booking.

01

Eligibility Requirements

Pilot Certification

  • All renters must possess a valid FAA (or equivalent aviation authority) pilot license appropriate for the aircraft class being rented
  • Minimum flight experience requirements vary by aircraft type (typically 200+ hours for single-engine, 1000+ hours for complex aircraft)
  • Recent flight experience: Minimum 15 hours within the last 90 days, including 3 takeoffs and landings
  • Valid medical certificate appropriate for the license type

Identification & Verification

  • Government-issued photo ID (passport or driver's license)
  • Current pilot license and medical certificate
  • Flight logbook for verification of recent experience
  • Credit card in the pilot's name for security deposit

Failure to provide complete documentation will result in rental denial without refund of reservation fees.

02

Rental Agreement

Binding Agreement

  • This agreement becomes effective upon signing the rental contract and payment of all required fees
  • By signing, the pilot-in-command accepts full responsibility for the aircraft and all persons aboard
  • The pilot-in-command warrants they have read and understand the aircraft's Pilot Operating Handbook (POH)
  • Rental terms cannot be modified without written approval from Veloce Aviation management

Rental Period

  • Rental time is calculated from engine start to engine shutdown
  • Minimum rental period is 1 hour, with additional time billed in 15-minute increments
  • Reservations must be canceled at least 24 hours in advance for full refund of reservation fee
  • No-shows or late cancellations (less than 24 hours) forfeit the reservation fee

The pilot-in-command is responsible for conducting a thorough pre-flight inspection and verifying the aircraft's airworthiness before each flight.

03

Financial Terms

Pricing & Payment

  • All rates are quoted in INR and include standard operating costs but exclude fuel
  • Fuel is billed at current market rates plus a 10% service fee
  • Payment methods: Credit card (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), wire transfer, or company check (with approved credit)
  • Reservation requires 25% deposit, with full payment due 72 hours prior to rental

Security Deposit

  • Security deposit of ₹50,000 required for single-engine aircraft, ₹200,000 for multi-engine aircraft
  • Deposit is held on your credit card and released within 14 business days after rental completion
  • Deposit may be used to cover damage, excessive wear, or violation of rental terms
  • No deposit is required for corporate accounts with established credit

Damage Liability

  • Pilot is responsible for all damage to the aircraft, regardless of cause
  • Deductible for insurance claims is ₹50,000 per incident for single-engine aircraft
  • Deductible increases to ₹200,000 for multi-engine and complex aircraft
  • Intentional damage or operation outside aircraft limitations voids insurance coverage
04

Operational Requirements

Flight Planning & Restrictions

  • All flights must be filed with appropriate ATC authorities where required
  • Maximum range limitations based on aircraft type and fuel capacity
  • Minimum weather requirements: VFR - 3SM visibility, 1000' ceiling; IFR requires instrument rating
  • International flights require special authorization and additional documentation

Weather Minimums

  • VFR minimums: 3 statute miles visibility, 1,000 feet ceiling (basic)
  • Special VFR requires specific clearance from ATC
  • Night flying requires additional experience and may have higher weather minimums
  • Pilot must cancel flights if weather falls below minimums or creates unsafe conditions

Navigation & Equipment

  • Required equipment must be operational: Altimeter, Airspeed Indicator, Compass, etc.
  • GPS navigation systems must have current database
  • ELT (Emergency Locator Transmitter) must be operational and registered
  • Portable oxygen required for flights above 12,500 feet for more than 30 minutes
05

Additional Terms

Passenger Policy

  • Pilot is responsible for all passengers and their safety during the flight
  • Maximum passenger capacity based on aircraft seating and weight limitations
  • Passengers must receive safety briefing before each flight
  • Children under 2 must be secured in FAA-approved child restraint systems

Prohibited Activities

  • Aerobatic maneuvers are strictly prohibited without special authorization
  • Flight into known icing conditions is prohibited
  • Carrying hazardous materials is strictly prohibited
  • Alcohol or drug use within 8 hours of flight (or longer as required by regulations)

Documentation & Reporting

  • Pilot must complete and sign flight log after each rental
  • Any discrepancies or damage must be reported immediately
  • Flight plans must be filed where required by regulations
  • Post-flight inspection must be documented in the aircraft logbook

These terms and conditions are subject to change without notice. The most current version will always be available on our website and provided at time of booking.
