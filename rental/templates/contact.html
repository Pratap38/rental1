Frequently Asked

Questions & Answers

Find quick answers to common questions about our services and policies.

What documents do I need to rent a car?

To rent a vehicle, you'll need a valid driver's license, government-issued photo ID (Aadhaar card, passport, or voter ID), and a security deposit payment method. International customers need an International Driving Permit along with their home country license.

What is your cancellation policy?

We offer free cancellation up to 48 hours before your scheduled pickup time. Cancellations within 48 hours incur a 25% fee of the total booking amount. No-shows will be charged the full booking amount.

Do you offer one-way rentals?

Yes, we offer one-way rentals between major cities. Additional charges may apply based on distance and vehicle type. Please contact our booking department for specific pricing on one-way rentals.

What is included in the rental price?

Our rental price includes the vehicle, basic insurance, roadside assistance, and 24/7 customer support. Additional services like GPS, child seats, and additional insurance coverage are available at extra cost.
